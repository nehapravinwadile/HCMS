Hostel Complaint Management

Staying in hostels can be the biggest adventure of your travels.

Hostel

Student residences are available on the Institute campus with varied sharing occupancy. Majority of the rooms have balconies that provide a good view of the surrounding area and a place for relaxation and interaction. Students can avail the on campus hostel facility..

Infrastructure

The institute has separate hostels for boys and girls. The girls hostel is allocated 300 beds to accommodate the residential requirements of girl students while 580 beds are allocated for the boys residences. The hostel rooms are comfortable with all basic amenities and allotted on a first-come-first-serve basis..

Parents accomodation

Parents Accommodation – Parents can book a room for a maximum of two nights This is available only during the hostel reporting period and can be availed by father/mother along with the student’s brother/sister. The charges for the same would be Rs. 500/- .

About Hostel
